Cinnamon Vanilla Latte

Ingredients

  • Espresso
  • Steamed milk
  • Cinnamon Powder
  • Vanilla sirup

Instructions

  1. Brew 1 shot of espresso.
  2. Steam 1 cup of milk.
  3. Add 1 tsp of vanilla syrup to the milk.
  4. Pour milk over espresso, psinkle with Cinnamon
